Frequently asked questions

OUR PLATFORM
How do online piano classes stack up to in-person learning?
The Pixical platform is designed to elevate your piano lessons beyond traditional in-person or virtual lessons in a number of ways:
  • Multiple camera angles make it easy for students to follow along as teachers demonstrate new techniques, mirroring an in-person learning experience.
  • Access to the best piano teachers in the world, from the comfort of your own home. You’re not limited by distance, availability, or scheduling conflicts.
  • Class notes, recordings, and resources are available for after-class review and practice - any time, anywhere.
What age group are Pixical piano classes designed for?
There’s no age limit on learning! Students of all ages and experience levels are welcome.
    What do I need before I sign up for a class?
    Getting started with Pixical is easy! All you need is:
    • A keyboard or piano
    • A laptop with a Chrome browser
    • A reliable internet connection
    ABOUT OUR TEACHERS
    Are your teachers comfortable with online teaching?
    Yes. Our teachers have years of experience with virtual teaching. Integrated instructional tools in the Pixical platform give our teachers a big advantage over traditional video conferencing platforms - letting them stay focused on you, instead of tinkering with their tech.
      How do you vet your teachers?
      All our piano teachers have a music degree. We verify their professional background and select those with experience with teaching online, with a love for lifelong learning.
